FAQ's

How do I track my steps?

Our app automatically tracks your steps using the built-in sensors on your device. Just keep your phone with you while walking.

How can I monitor my hydration levels?

You can log your water intake manually in the app, and it will remind you to drink water at regular intervals.

Can I track my sleep patterns?

Yes, our app can track your sleep patterns if you wear a compatible device while sleeping.

How do I measure my heart rate?

You can measure your heart rate using a compatible heart rate monitor or a smartwatch that syncs with our app.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously and use advanced encryption methods to ensure your data is secure.
